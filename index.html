Who we are

Cadena is an initiative by Inclusion, a New York organization founded in 2015 that provides skills and career training in web development. Our mission with Cadena is to provide software engineers with a high quality curriculum, a supportive community, and the career skills required to become a blockchain developer.

Our instructors come from some of the top companies in web3 like VaynerNFT and ConsenSys and our graduates have been hired by companies like Gemini, Tesla, Google, Amazon, Wayfair, Rent The Runway, and WeWork. We are supported by industry leading companies like Twilio, Goldman Sachs, HP, and Slack.
